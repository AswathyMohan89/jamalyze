What We Found

Entry Barrier

For those who want to attend the most concerts, become a fan of Dance/Electronic music. In our sample, Dance/Electronic had the lowest average event price at $28.89, while Pop events had the highest at $54.55. However, on the other side, it was not Pop events that had the highest top end price. That distinction belongs to Rock 'n' Roll.

Yanni's Delight

Enjoy the sultry sounds of Yanni? Enough to spend $3200? Yanni's 25th Acropolis Anniversary tour came in with the most expensive ticket. His show in Prior Lake, MN, includes a meet and greet, a premium merchandise ensemble, and a private suite. Enjoy Yanni but not at that price? Tickets only start at $40.

Sin City

Nevada might not be the first state you associate with Pop culture, but the state attracts Pop music. Thanks to Las Vegas and artists having standing engagements at many venues, Sin City has more to offer than just casinos. For instance, don't miss out on Frank Marino's Divas, a live show of celebrity female impersonators!

Country Time

California, with it's size and year-round beautiful weather, is a hot spot for many events. The state has the most Alternative, Rock, Hip-Hop/Rap, and even Metal events across the country. But what does it lack? Tennessee's desire and love for country music. Thanks to the epicenter of Country music in Nashville, Tenneessee will host nearly 100 shows this year.
